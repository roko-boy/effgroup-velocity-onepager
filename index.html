Phase 1
Discovery — Getting Aligned
Before we build anything, we need to align on inputs. Here's what we'll cover to ensure every program is calibrated for your business.
📐 Velocity Definition
What does "winning" look like in 60–90 days? Units sold, repeat rate, priority retailers, priority regions — let's define it together.
📊 Current Rebates + Performance
What's running today? What's worked, what hasn't? We'll audit existing programs before recommending anything new.
🏷️ Brand Prioritization
Which brands need velocity support urgently? We'll tier the portfolio and stage rollout accordingly.
🛒 SKU + Retailer Focus
Any hero SKUs or priority retailers to lead with? We'll build offers around what matters most to your buyers.
💰 Offer Economics Guardrails
Incentive caps, exclusions, preferred value thresholds. We'll recommend 20%+ of product value where feasible — but we'll work within your guardrails.

Who gets reports, how often, and on what KPIs? Weekly or monthly cadence — we'll set this up before anything goes live.

Desired output: Prioritized brand rollout plan · Evergreen offer plan per brand · Scale rules (where we push harder vs. hold)

The Program
Six Levers. Built for Your Portfolio.
Each program is modular — you can activate all of them or start with the ones that matter most right now.
📦

Out-of-Stock Reporting (Stocky) Start Here

Before we scale any promotion, we need shelf confidence. Stocky gives Eagle Brands automated OOS visibility across retailers — so you can fix issues before wasting spend on an empty shelf.

  • Automated OOS alerts to your team (+ sales team if desired)
  • Faster issue resolution, better retailer relationships
  • Ensures velocity programs scale only where they can convert
  • Prevents wasted promotional spend against unavailable product
🗺️

Store Locator + Reporting Hub

The store locator becomes your conversion and learning engine — not just a static "where to buy" page. We'll operationalize it with weekly/monthly reporting so you always know where intent is concentrated.

  • Locator views, clicks, top geos, and device breakdown
  • Identify friction points (high views, low clicks)
  • Guide where offers, targeting, and budget should scale
  • Pinpoint velocity gaps by retailer and geography
💸

Evergreen Offers Core Lever

At least one always-on rebate per brand — available across all retailers, featured on the store locator, designed to generate consistent learnings and repeatable ROI.

  • Always-on, scalable, built for sustained velocity
  • Target 20%+ consumer value (baseline recommendation)
  • Target 2–3× ROI where possible
  • Generates learnings that compound over time
🌐

Aisle Community Site

List your evergreen offers on the Aisle community site for incremental volume at zero additional cost. You pay only for redemptions — already included in your subscription.

  • No additional cost to list
  • Pay only for redemptions (included in subscription)
  • Incremental reach beyond your own audience
  • Visibility to Aisle's existing shopper community
🔁

Retention + Re-engagement

Turn new signups into repeat buyers. We'll build automated re-engagement tracks so contacts don't go dark after their first offer — segmented by brand, geo, retailer, and engagement level.

  • Automated repeat-reward and loyalty-style tracks
  • One-off push campaigns via mass message when needed
  • Segmentation by brand, geo, retailer, and engagement
  • Defined re-engagement plan before any offer goes live
📋

Insight Surveys (When Needed)

For brands that need deeper consumer visibility, we'll deploy short 5–7 question surveys to capture what's driving — or blocking — purchase decisions.

  • Repeat purchase intent
  • Drivers and barriers to trial
  • Innovation direction signals
  • In-store experience feedback
⚡

Optional Accelerator: Paid Media

Paid is a force multiplier — but only once we have in-stock confidence and a clear velocity motion. We won't recommend paid until Stocky + evergreen are in place and performing.

Routing Options

  • Drive to Eagle's store locator (Eagle controls the experience)
  • Drive to an Aisle page (fastest conversion + list growth)

When It Makes Sense

  • Launching at a new retailer? Paid gets people in the door.
  • Supporting a hero SKU? Paid drives trial with clear attribution.
  • Proving velocity to a buyer? Paid + Aisle = verifiable data.

Execution Plan
0–90 Day Roadmap
A phased approach that builds on itself — foundation first, then optimization, then scale.
0–30 Days · Foundation
Build the Base
  • Discovery call → align on brands, SKUs, retailers, economics
  • Stand up OOS reporting (Stocky)
  • Activate store locator reporting cadence
  • Launch first evergreen offer(s) on locator + community site

  • Optimize evergreen offers based on early data

  • Monthly business review — review KPIs vs. targets
61–90 Days · Scale
Double Down
  • Add targeted retailer/SKU programs based on data
  • Scale what's working; pause what isn't
  • Expand to remaining brands in portfolio
  • Formalize ongoing monthly business review cadence
  • Build case study + velocity proof for buyers
